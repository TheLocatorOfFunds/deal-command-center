About Our Company

A Title Company Built on Relationships, Not Transactions

Southeast Indiana Title, Inc. is a locally-owned title agency proudly serving Batesville and the surrounding Southeast Indiana region. We've earned our reputation one closing at a time — by treating every buyer, seller, lender, agent, and auctioneer like a neighbor, because here, they usually are.

From residential closings to complex commercial transactions, farm sales, and auction settlements, our team delivers the diligence of a big-city firm with the personal attention only a hometown company can provide.

  • Attorney-owned and operated for over two decades
  • Comprehensive title searches backed by local expertise
  • Trusted closing agent for regional auctions & farm sales
  • Referenced in ALTA (American Land Title Association) industry publications
  • Members of the Batesville Area Chamber of Commerce

Meet Our Founder

Douglas C. Amberger

Attorney at Law · Owner

Doug Amberger brings over 25 years of Indiana legal experience to every transaction that crosses his desk. Admitted to the Indiana State Bar in 2000 and a resident member in good standing, Doug built Southeast Indiana Title, Inc. on a foundation of thoroughness, integrity, and genuine care for the community he calls home.

Beyond the title company, Doug serves as an appointed member of the City of Batesville Planning Commission and Board of Zoning Appeals, and as President of Golden Villa, a long-established local nonprofit. He's also a regular contributor to Batesville Area Chamber of Commerce programs on elder law and estate planning.

How It Works

Your Path to a Smooth Closing

A transparent, step-by-step approach so you always know where things stand.

1

Order Opened

Your agent, lender, or attorney opens a title order with our office. We confirm the details and get started right away.

2

Title Searched

Our team conducts a comprehensive search of public records to produce a complete title commitment.

3

Issues Cleared

We work with all parties to resolve any liens, judgments, or exceptions before your closing date.

4

Closing Day

Sign your documents, receive your policy, and walk away with full ownership — and total peace of mind.
